Why an Ethics Hotline

You don’t want to be the last to know of unethical activity in your organisation. Information from staff and suppliers is a valuable source of information about wrongdoing and risk. For some, a trusted hotline will be the first, the last or the only method they will use to share critical insights with you. It’s not surprising then that good governance and legal compliance requires you to offer reliable channels by which safe reports can be made. In fact, it’s now such an important part of your risk management strategy that operating an ethics hotline has become a highly specialised service.
